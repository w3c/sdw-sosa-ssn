Complex devices

Many sensors and actuators are packaged with other sensors and actuators in a complex device. For example, the consumer grade Inkbird IBS-TH2-PLUS has a temperature and relative-humidity sensor in a single package. The sosa:System class provides for this using the sosa:hasSubSystem property.
