Generic or Specific Instances of sosa:Property

The previous edition of SSN [[vocab-ssn-20171019]] left an ambiguity on whether an instance of sosa:Property should be generic to all features of interest (e.g., ex:Temperature, ex:OnOffStatus), or specific to a single feature of interest (e.g., <myBodyTemperature>, <LightStatus>). This version solves the ambiguity by differentiating:

  • sosa:Property for the former case, where properties are generic
  • sosa:PropertyOfInterest for the latter case, where properties are tied to a single entity.
