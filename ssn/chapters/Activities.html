Activities Module

Observations and Samplings are typically undertaken as part of larger activities, often referred to as projects, initiatives, campaigns and similar. These activities will commonly include many observations and samplings, and also actuations, related to a systematic study. The Activities Module is a lightweight extension to the SSN Ontology that supports collections of the various kinds of execution.

A sosa-act:Activity collects sosa:Actuations, sosa:Observations, sosa:Samplings, sosa:ActuationCollections, sosa:ObservationCollections, and sosa:SamplingCollections. sosa-act:Activitys can be nested recursively to describe additional levels of organization, such as campaigns that are part of projects, that are in turn part of initiatives.

The namespace for Activities terms is http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/activity/

The suggested prefix for the Activities namespace is sosa-act

The Activities graph is available at http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/activity/.
