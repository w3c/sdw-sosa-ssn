SAREF Alignment Module

The ETSI Smart Applications REFerence (SAREF) suite of ontologies ([[SAREF]]) forms a shared model of consensus intended to enable semantic interoperability between solutions from different providers and among various activity sectors in the Internet of Things (IoT), thus contributing to the development of data spaces.

The ETSI portal for SAREF exposes the SAREF ontologies and points to the different SAREF-related deliverables, which are open standards produced by ETSI Technical Committee Smart Machine-to-Machine communications (SmartM2M).

Since V2.1.1, SAREF and SSN have common contributors, who work on the convergence of these reference ontologies. This section introduces the alignment of SOSA to to SAREF V3.2.1 [[SAREF]] and SAREF4SYST [[SAREF_Patterns]].

An RDF file containing a graph corresponding to this alignment is available.

Namespaces

The following namespace prefixes are used in the alignment of SOSA to SAREF.

sosa: http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/
